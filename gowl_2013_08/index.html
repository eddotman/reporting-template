Monthly Status Report


August 2013

Overview

Startup Canada has been working in partnership with Gowlings to support entrepreneurs in Canada through a variety of platforms, including Facebook posts, #StartupChats hosted on Twitter, and articles featured in PIVOT Magazine.

Highlights

#StartupChats

On 28 August 2013, Startup Canada hosted a #StartupChats session with Jamie Hollingworth on the topic of sole proprietorship vs. incorporation. Dozens of followers were engaged in the conversation, and a summary of the chat can be seen on Storify.

Summary

Through the partnership between Gowlings and Startup Canada, a consistently growing number of entrepreneurs have been reached through the social media campaigns hosted on Twitter and Facebook. As evidenced by the #StartupChats session with Jamie Hollingworth as well as Startup Canada's steady growth of influence on Facebook, Startup Canada and Gowlings have made steady progress towards promoting entrepreneurship in Canada.
