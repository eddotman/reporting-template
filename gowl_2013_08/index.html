Monthly Status Report


August 2013

Overview

Startup Canada has been working in partnership with Gowlings to support entrepreneurs in Canada through a variety of programming including Facebook posts, #StartupChats hosted on Twitter, events and articles featured in PIVOT Magazine.

As a Silver sponsor, Gowlings can expect the following commitments from Startup Canada:

  • Promotion of Gowlings via the Startup Canada website.
  • Rotating banner on website.
  • Logo and link on the sponsors page of the Startup Canada website.
  • Recognition in all press releases as a sponsor of Startup Canada.
  • Semi annually promoting Gowlings service offers through website and social media channels.
  • Opportunity to provide expert articles and blogs to the Startup Canada blog.
  • Quarterly #startupchat featuring Gowlings and continued Twitter promotion of Gowlings thought leadership.
  • Promotion of Gowlings in Startup Canada Membership Welcome packages.
  • Opportunity for Gowlings to provide materials and offerings to Startup Canada members.

Highlights

#StartupChats

On 28 August 2013, Startup Canada hosted a #StartupChats session with Jamie Hollingworth of Gowlings on the topic of sole proprietorship vs. incorporation. Over 585 000 Twitter impressions were made during the #StartupChats session, and a summary of the chat can be seen on Storify.
