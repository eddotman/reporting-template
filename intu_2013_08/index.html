Sponsors Report: Intuit

August 2013

Objective

In partnership with Intuit, Startup Canada has worked to deliver top-tier media coverage and to generate a measurable increase to social media engagement, impressions, and shares. The intent is to produce public relations and social media content that enables both parties to grow brand awareness by demonstrating unique insights into how best to boost entrepreneurship in Canada.
