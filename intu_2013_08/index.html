Project Completion Report


August 2013

Overview

Earlier this year, Intuit Canada talked to more than 500 small business owners about the top trends and challenges of job creation and sourcing top talent. Spurred by the results of that study, Startup Canada and Intuit Canada have partnered on a campaign to support small business owners and entrepreneurs across Canada in accessing the knowledge and tools they need for better hiring practices and to support the success and growth of their companies. From Twitter Chats and articles to videos and hangouts, this campaign has been helping Startups to hire all summer long.

Objectives

  1. Pilot a joint Intuit - Startup Canada Campaign on social media;
  2. Increase engagement with Intuit's new Google+ Channel; and,
  3. Continue to position Intuit as a thought leader in Canadian entrepreneurship.
