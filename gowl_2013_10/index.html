Monthly Status Report


October 2013

Overview

Startup Canada has been working in partnership with Gowlings to support entrepreneurs in Canada through a variety of programming including Facebook posts, #StartupChats hosted on Twitter, events and articles featured in PIVOT Magazine.

As a Silver sponsor, Gowlings can expect the following commitments from Startup Canada:

  • Promotion of Gowlings via the Startup Canada website.
  • Rotating banner on website.
  • Logo and link on the sponsors page of the Startup Canada website.
  • Recognition in all press releases as a sponsor of Startup Canada.
  • Semi annually promoting Gowlings service offers through website and social media channels.
  • Opportunity to provide expert articles and blogs to the Startup Canada blog.
  • Quarterly #startupchat featuring Gowlings and continued Twitter promotion of Gowlings thought leadership.
  • Promotion of Gowlings in Startup Canada Membership Welcome packages.
  • Opportunity for Gowlings to provide materials and offerings to Startup Canada members.

Highlights

#StartupChats

On 23 October 2013, Startup Canada hosted a #StartupChats session with Rene Bissonette of Gowlings on the topic of social media legal risks. A summary of the chat can be seen on Storify. The official description of the #StartupChats session is provided below:

Social Media can be a fantastic, cost effective way to market your business, yet how much do we really know about what legal risks can be involved? In this educational chat we will determine some best practices for social media to avoid legal risks.

Summary

Startup Canada continues to work on promoting Gowlings to entrepreneurs through its social media channels. Through the partnership of Gowlings and Startup Canada, a growing number of entrepreneurs have been reached through the social media campaigns hosted on Twitter, LinkedIn and Facebook. This month, the #StartupChats session with Rene Bissonette was used to give attention to important entrepreneurial issues and position Gowlings as a thought leader on social media.
